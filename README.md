# Mindphor

> Customer Intelligence & Competitor Watch

Mindphor automatically monitors customer feedback, app ratings, and competitor feature releases in real time using AI. Aggregates Google Play, App Store, Twitter, and Reddit reviews so you can understand your users and keep tabs on competitors.

---

## Features

- **Feedback Aggregation** — automatically pulls reviews from App Store, Google Play, Twitter, and Reddit.
- **AI Sentiment Analysis** — extracts reviews and sentiment scores, categorizing feedback dynamically.
- **Competitor Watch** — track competitor feature drops, pricing updates, and user complaints.
- **Customer Alignment** — three steps to alignment: Connect channels, AI Sentiment Analysis, and Competitor Watch.
